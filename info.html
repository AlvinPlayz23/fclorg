About FCL.ORG

FCL.ORG, founded in Kuwait, is dedicated to promoting street football. We host annual tournaments and leagues, offering a platform for local talent to shine. Our mission is to foster community spirit and provide opportunities for players to showcase their skills and passion for football.

FCL Champions League

The FCL Champions League is our premier tournament, bringing together top teams to compete for the ultimate title in street football. It is a highly anticipated event where teams battle it out in intense matches, aiming to claim the prestigious FCL Champions League trophy.

FCL League

The FCL League is an annual competition where teams vie for supremacy over a season, showcasing their skills and determination. The league format allows for consistent performance to be rewarded, with teams competing in a series of matches to earn the top spot in the standings.
